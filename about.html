About Me — Klement Gunndu

CCNA Engineer | GenAI Engineer | Building AI for the Real World

I used to keep networks alive. Now I build AI that works in the real world.

My Journey

I started as a CCNA-certified network engineer, working in NOC teams, ensuring Fortune 500 systems stayed online. I mastered performance tools like JMeter, LoadRunner, Dynatrace, and AppDynamics — because in infrastructure, reliability isn’t optional.

Then came AI. And I saw a gap: most AI lives in the cloud, disconnected from physical reality. So I set out to bridge digital intelligence with real-world action.

What I Build Now

Today, I’m a GenAI Engineer focused on:

  • • Edge AI — running models on-device for low-latency, private inference
  • • Multi-Agent Systems — coordinated AI agents for complex workflows
  • • Physical AI — integrating AI with robotics, sensors, and automation
  • • RAG & Fine-tuning — for accurate, domain-specific AI

I don’t just prototype — I deploy. Because AI should work like infrastructure: reliable, traceable, and trustworthy.

Who I Work With

I’m open to opportunities everywhere:

  • • Big Tech — building scalable GenAI infrastructure
  • • Startups & Research Labs — pioneering edge AI and robotics
  • • Local Businesses — helping them survive the AI shift through GEO

My dream? To build AI systems that don’t just answer questions — but act in the world.
